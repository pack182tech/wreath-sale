# Zelle QR Code

Place your Zelle payment QR code here.

## Requirements:
- **Format:** PNG or JPG
- **Size:** 500x500 pixels (square)
- **Quality:** Must be scannable - test with your phone camera!
- **Crop:** Remove app interface, just the QR code

## File Name:
- `zelle-qr-code.png`

## How to Get Your Zelle QR Code:
1. Open your Zelle app
2. Go to "Request Money" or your profile
3. Find your QR code
4. Screenshot or download it
5. Crop to just the QR code
6. Save as `zelle-qr-code.png`

## Important:
- Test scanning the QR code with your phone before using it
- Make sure it's clear and not blurry
- The QR code should link to your pack's Zelle account

See `/ASSET_PLACEMENT_GUIDE.md` for complete instructions.
